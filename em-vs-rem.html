Em vs Rem

Experiment with the effects of mixing rem and em units by setting the font size of the component divs and seeing the effects on children. Elements sized with rem units are generally not affected by changing the font size of their parent, but elements sized with em units are. The only way to have a cascading affect on font size of elements sized with rem units is to change the font size of the :root pseudo class or the html element itself, as :root and html are at the very tippy top of the document object model. Which is better, em or rem? You will find articles online that advocate for one or the other, but they all generally acknowledge that "it depends." This is a somewhat advanced and opinionated topic for most learners, so I encourage you to defer learning more about this, and, for now, when in doubt, just be consistent and, whether you use em units or rem units. As you advance, you will find that em units are great for the children of resizable components. If you'd like to learn more, I recommend the great article about em vs rem by LogRocket.
